Hello there and welcome to my profile :D

All right, about me: I'm a girl in my twenties, I'm a translator, and I was Sorted into Slytherin on Pottermore.

I'm a passionate Harry Potter fan, I absolutely love it! It's also the only fandom I write fanfiction for. I don't read anymore, usually, only if something really seizes me and only if it is canon-like or completely AU ... not so many of those out there :D I can only recommend A Year In The Life and Stealing Harry. They're perfect.

My favourite pairings are: SS/RL, RL/NT, RL/MM, KS/SS, KS/RL but all in all I am fine with everything concerning Remus and/or Severus as long as it's well written and believable.

So, just enjoy my fanfiction if you like and please review, I'd love to know what you think. Thanks!
